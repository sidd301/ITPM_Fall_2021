What is Apex Legends?

Apex legends is a battle royale game with either a 20 three-person squads or 30 two-person duos. These duos/trios land on an island and search for weapons/supplies before eliminating other players on the island.

What Makes Apex Legends Unique to the Battle Royale Genre?

Unlike popular Battle Royales such as Fortnite or PUBG, Apex Legends utilizes a unique movement system that resembles parkour. Therefore, the player is able to move around the buildings/terrain in a very unique way.

Apex Legends Characters

BloodHound

BloodHound is one of the most iconic characters within Apex Legends. This character is known as a Technological Tracker because of their ability to see the footsteps and markings of enemies near them. BloodHound utilizes a form of scanning technology to spot enemies at a distance. "I am the hunter the Gods have sent."

Gibraltar

Gibraltar or popularily known as Gibby is one of the main defensive/tank characters within the Apex Legends universe. This character is known for his bubble which is a shield that he can throw near him. His ultimate is also one of the most lethal ultimates within the game as he will throw down a gernade looking object which signals an airstrike to rain down on his enemies. "Try to move me; it'll be fun."

Bangalore

Bangalore is known as one of the best skirmishing and dueling characters within the game. She excels at disrupting enemies with her smoke-screen and her high mobility through the use of her passive which grants her bonus movement speed when in combat. She pairs quite well with Bloodhound through their smoke-screen and scan tactic. Her ultimate is similar to Gibraltar's, however it is a weaker version. "Name the weapon--I'll still beat you."

Octane

Octane is widely recognized as one of the fastest characters in Apex Legends. His title is the High-Speed Daredevil and he lives up to his name. He uses a stim injection to move 30% faster for six seconds, while his ultimate is throwing a launchpad infront of him for his teammates to use. This character is incredibly mobile and can reposition himself in fights extremely quickly. "Whoa... what a rush!"
